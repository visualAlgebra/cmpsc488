Welcome to Visual Algebra

Our project members are dedicated to create a new and interesting algebra revolving around visual graphics and simple to understand operations. Our (not yet) implemented auto solving algorithm will help any user find the next step to complete their problem. Visit our explorer page to find new problems created by teachers or other lesson and problem creators! Please visit us at GitHub for problems with the webpage or any bugs you may find! GitHub Project
